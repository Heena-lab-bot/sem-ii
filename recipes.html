Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

2 cups pizza dough
1/2 cup tomato sauce
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1 tablespoon olive oil
Pepperoni slices
Onion slices
Bell peppers
Oregano and chili flakes

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll the pizza dough into a circular shape.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and desired toppings.
  5. Drizzle olive oil on top.
  6. Bake for 15–20 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  8. Slice and serve hot.
